Vehicle history report

Limited information is available for conditionally registered vehicles. Because the vehicle you have checked is conditionally registered, a vehicle history report is not available.

Important information

  • This report is not usable in court or certified for any legal evidence.
  • Not all information is available on all vehicles.
  • The vehicle check covers only NSW registered vehicles. For vehicles registered interstate, contact the registration authority in that state for the vehicle's history.
  • This report is not a confirmation of the vehicle's road worthiness or that it meets safety standards.

Buying a vehicle

  • Before buying a damaged vehicle, check if the insurance company intends to write it off. A decision to write it off could be made even after sale, and notification to Roads and Maritime Services takes time.
  • If you intend buying a vehicle from a private seller, you should visit the Personal Property Securities Register website to find out if there is any finance owing.
  • If you have been given this report by the seller of this vehicle, you may want to obtain your own report to check that the details of this vehicle have not changed, or to check any other vehicles that you may be interested in.
